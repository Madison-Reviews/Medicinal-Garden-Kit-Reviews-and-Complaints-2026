Deep Sleep and Tension Relief

Valerian root is among the most studied natural sleep support plants. Research suggests its active compounds (valerenic acid) enhance GABA activity and reduce the time to sleep onset. Unlike chamomile, valerian is primarily a root remedy that requires patience, as it establishes in year one and is harvested in year two or three. St. John's Wort
Mood and Nerve Support

One of the most studied botanical mood-support plants with an extensive clinical research database. Multiple systematic reviews indicate St. John's Wort is more effective than placebo for mild to moderate depression, with a side effect profile comparable to prescription antidepressants for mild cases. Important note: it has significant drug interactions and is not appropriate for everyone (see safety section).

Marshmallow Root
Digestive and Respiratory Soothing

Althaea officinalis contains mucilaginous polysaccharides that coat and soothe mucous membranes in the digestive and respiratory tracts. Research suggests marshmallow root preparations reduce irritation in conditions like gastritis, dry cough, and sore throat. It is a slow-growing perennial that requires patience but provides one of the more unique soothing remedies not easily found in commercial supplement form.

Important Note on St. John's Wort

St. John's Wort induces liver enzymes that accelerate the metabolism of many prescription medications, including oral contraceptives, antiretrovirals, blood thinners, and certain antidepressants. This is a clinically significant drug interaction, not a minor caution. Anyone taking prescription medications should consult their physician before preparing or using St. John's Wort from any source, including this kit.
